About product :

ISpine-dispensers is an IoT enabled smart sanitary napkin dispenser. Our circular and compact product and can fit into any workspace and dispenses sanitary napkins through various accessible modes(Qr scan,biometric scan,etc) customised for your needs.cOur robust system also gives an automatic restock alert for maintenance staff and performs continuous health monitoring to ensure full time service. We also provide Predictive analytics to understand and meet future demands efficiently.
