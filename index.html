Our Product

About product :

ISpine-dispensers is an IoT enabled smart sanitary napkin dispenser. Our circular and compact storage unit is compatible with all environments; be it workspaces, schools or malls. The unit dispenses sanitary napkins through various accessible modes, customized for your needs. Our robust system also gives an automatic restock alert for maintenance staff, and performs continuous health monitoring to ensure full time service. What more? We also provide predictive analytics to understand and meet future demands efficiently.
